Claw Grab — Design Notes

  • Pile layout: Items scattered organically with overlaps — feels like a real claw machine
  • Skill element: Position the claw precisely — it grabs the top-most item under the crosshair
  • Better rewards buried deeper at the bottom of the pile
  • Tries: 1 free/day + 2 RV (+1 each) + IAP bundles (5 or 10)
  • No gameplay loop — pure monetization event, time-limited from main menu
  • Crosshair line shows exactly where the claw will drop
